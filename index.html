RestOrRant

What Is This?

This is a simple restaurant management application intended to ease the management of restaurants for those who work in restaurants. This is a desktop application that has a GUI but most of the user interactions happen using a CLI (Command Line Interface). Hence, our target users are restaurant waiters and managers who can type fast and prefer typing over clicking on GUI.

What Can It Do?

It can…​

  • Display the current status of the restaurant at any time.

  • Manage the use of tables in the restaurant.

  • Track all orders for all tables in the restaurant.

  • Consolidate statistics of the restaurant.

    • Total revenue

    • Total number of customers

    • Order frequency of menu items
